Project Overview

The AI-Based Privacy Risk & Policy Analyzer is an intelligent web application developed to help users understand the privacy implications of website privacy policies. The system automatically extracts privacy policy content from a given website URL and applies AI and Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to analyze, summarize, and identify potential privacy risks. By simplifying lengthy and complex privacy policies, the application enables users to make more informed decisions regarding their personal data and online privacy.

The project incorporates multiple features, including privacy risk scoring, policy summarization, risk classification, infographic generation, audio-based explanations, and multilingual translation support. A Privacy Risk Dashboard visually presents detected risks, AI-generated scores, and policy insights, while a Chrome Extension allows users to access privacy analysis directly during web browsing. These features improve accessibility and help users quickly understand important privacy concerns without reading lengthy policy documents.
